DecideNow Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024/8/28

1. Introduction

DecideNow Mobile (hereinafter referred to as "the app") is provided by an individual developer. This privacy policy explains how information is collected, used, and disclosed in relation to the use of the app.

2. Information We Collect

The app does not collect personal information, but may collect the following information:

  • Decision items and evaluation criteria entered by users
  • Device type and language settings

3. Purpose of Information Use

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing app functionality and improving performance
  • Enhancing user experience
  • Analysis and disclosure of anonymized statistical data

4. Information Sharing

The developer does not share raw collected data with third parties. However, exceptions may be made in the following cases:

  • When required to comply with legal requests
  • When necessary to protect the developer's rights

5. Data Storage and Management

The app uses Google Firebase (hereinafter "Firebase") for storing and managing collected data. Firebase is a secure cloud-based platform provided by Google. Please note the following points:

  • Data is stored on Firebase servers and is encrypted.
  • We follow Firebase's security measures to prevent unauthorized access to data.
  • The location of data storage depends on Firebase server locations, which may be outside your country.
  • Firebase's privacy policy and Google's data processing terms apply.

While the use of Firebase allows the app to provide a more secure and efficient service, please understand that Google is involved in data management.

6. Data Security

The developer implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the collected information. This includes the use of Firebase's security features. Data is anonymized and stored in an encrypted state. However, as internet-based data transmission and electronic storage methods are not 100% secure, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Disclaimer

This app is a decision-making support tool, and the final judgment should be made at the user's own responsibility.

Please note that the developer and operator cannot be held responsible for any damages or disadvantages resulting from the use of this app.

8. Advertising

The app displays advertisements. We use Google AdMob for ad delivery, which may use anonymous advertising IDs. The collection and use of data related to advertising comply with Google's advertising policies.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

The developer may update this privacy policy from time to time. When changes are made, we will notify you through the app and provide the latest information on this page. In case of significant changes, we may seek your consent again.
